## 💬 Getting Help
If you have questions, problems, or need guidance:

1. **Search existing issues**  
   Check the [Issues](../issues) page to see if your question or problem has already been discussed.

2. **Open a new issue**  
   If you don’t find an existing answer, open a new issue and provide as much detail as possible:
   - Steps to reproduce
   - Expected vs. actual behavior
   - Version information (if relevant)

3. **Discussions (if enabled)**  
   Use the [Discussions](../discussions) tab for general questions, ideas, or community conversation.

---

## 🛠️ Common Problems
- **Installation errors** → Make sure dependencies are installed and versions match requirements.
- **Build failures** → Check the README’s build section and ensure your environment matches the prerequisites.
- **Usage questions** → Look at provided examples or open a discussion thread.
